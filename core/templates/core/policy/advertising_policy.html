Advertising Policy

Last updated: April 2025

Contents:

1. Introduction

At Show Your Spot, we strive to offer our services while maintaining a sustainable business model. To do this, we incorporate various forms of advertising across our platform. This Advertising Policy explains how we handle advertising on Show Your Spot, including the types of ads we display, how we work with advertising partners, and the controls you have over your advertising experience.

This Advertising Policy should be read alongside our Privacy Policy and Cookies Policy, which provide more information about how we collect and use your data, including for advertising purposes.

2. Types of Advertising

Show Your Spot utilizes various forms of advertising across our platform:

2.1 Display Advertisements

These are visual advertisements displayed in designated areas of our website, including banners, sidebars, and in-content placements. These ads may be static images, animated graphics, or interactive media.

2.2 Affiliate Links

We may include special links to products or services from third-party retailers or service providers. When you click on these links and make a purchase, we may earn a commission from the retailer or service provider.

2.3 Sponsored Content

This includes articles, posts, or features that are created in collaboration with advertisers or sponsors. All sponsored content is clearly labeled as such to maintain transparency.

2.4 Promotional Emails

If you've opted to receive our newsletter or marketing communications, these may include promotional content or offers from our partners.

3. Third-Party Advertisers

We work with third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information about your visits to our website and other websites to provide relevant advertisements about goods and services that may interest you.

3.1 Our Advertising Partners

We currently work with the following advertising networks and platforms:

  • Google AdSense
  • Google Ad Manager
  • Facebook Audience Network
  • Amazon Advertising

Each of these partners maintains their own privacy and advertising policies. We encourage you to review these policies for more information about how these companies collect and use your data.

3.2 Data Sharing with Advertisers

We may share certain information with our advertising partners to facilitate the delivery of relevant advertisements:

  • Device information (such as your device type, operating system, and browser)
  • IP address and approximate location (typically at the city or postal code level)
  • Browsing activity on our website
  • Demographic information (when provided by you or inferred)

We do not share your personal contact information (like your name or email address) with advertisers without your explicit consent.

4. Affiliate Marketing

Show Your Spot participates in affiliate marketing programs, including but not limited to the Amazon Associates Program.

4.1 How Affiliate Marketing Works

When you click on an affiliate link on our website and subsequently make a purchase on the third-party website, we may earn a commission from that sale. These links do not change the price you pay for products or services.

4.2 Affiliate Link Disclosure

We are committed to transparency in our affiliate marketing practices. Affiliate links or content will be clearly disclosed with statements such as:

  • "This post contains affiliate links. We may earn a commission if you make a purchase."
  • "As an Amazon Associate, we earn from qualifying purchases."

4.3 Product Recommendations

Our product recommendations and reviews are based on genuine opinions and experiences. We strive to provide honest, unbiased information about products and services, regardless of affiliate relationships.

5. Sponsored Content

We occasionally publish sponsored content on our platform, which is content that has been paid for by an advertiser.

5.1 Sponsored Content Disclosure

All sponsored content on Show Your Spot is clearly labeled with disclosures such as:

  • "Sponsored Post"
  • "Promoted by [Brand Name]"
  • "In Partnership with [Brand Name]"

These disclosures are prominently displayed at the beginning of the content.

5.2 Editorial Standards for Sponsored Content

While sponsored content is produced in collaboration with advertisers, we maintain editorial control and ensure that all content:

  • Is relevant and valuable to our audience
  • Meets our quality standards
  • Is factually accurate
  • Clearly distinguishes between facts and opinions

6. Ad Targeting and Personalization

To make advertising more relevant to you, we and our advertising partners may use various targeting and personalization technologies.

6.1 Types of Targeting

Advertisements may be targeted based on:

  • Contextual targeting: Ads related to the content you're viewing
  • Behavioral targeting: Ads based on your browsing history and interactions
  • Demographic targeting: Ads based on age, gender, and other demographic factors
  • Geographic targeting: Ads based on your approximate location
  • Interest-based targeting: Ads based on inferred interests from your online activities

6.2 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Advertising targeting relies on cookies and similar tracking technologies. For detailed information about how we use these technologies, please review our Cookies Policy.

7. Your Choices and Controls

We respect your preferences regarding advertising and provide several ways for you to control your advertising experience.

7.1 Opting Out of Personalized Advertising

You can opt out of personalized advertising through:

  • Our cookie consent tool available on our website
  • Digital Advertising Alliance (DAA) opt-out tool
  • Network Advertising Initiative (NAI) opt-out tool
  • European Interactive Digital Advertising Alliance (EDAA) opt-out tool (for users in the EU)

Please note that opting out of personalized advertising does not mean you will see fewer ads. Instead, the ads you see will be less relevant to your interests.

7.2 Ad Blockers

You may choose to use ad-blocking software to prevent advertisements from displaying on our website. However, we kindly ask you to consider whitelisting our site, as advertising revenue helps us continue to provide free content and services.

7.3 Marketing Communications

You can opt out of marketing emails that may contain promotional content by:

  • Clicking the "unsubscribe" link in any marketing email we send
  • Updating your communication preferences in your account settings
  • Contacting us directly at privacy@showyourspot.com

8. Prohibited Advertising Content

We do not allow advertisements on our platform that:

  • Promote illegal products, services, or activities
  • Contain sexually explicit or adult content
  • Promote tobacco, vaping, or illegal drugs
  • Contain hate speech or discriminatory content
  • Contain violent or graphic content
  • Make misleading or false claims
  • Infringe on intellectual property rights
  • Contain malware, spyware, or other harmful code
  • Promote get-rich-quick schemes or questionable financial practices
  • Target children inappropriately

We reserve the right to refuse or remove any advertisement that violates these guidelines or that we deem inappropriate for our audience.

9. Ad Quality Standards

We strive to maintain a positive user experience by ensuring that advertisements on our platform:

  • Do not significantly impact page load times
  • Do not use overly intrusive formats (such as full-screen overlays or auto-playing videos with sound)
  • Are clearly distinguishable from editorial content
  • Do not mimic site functionality or display fake alerts
  • Are responsive and display properly across devices

We continuously monitor ad quality and user feedback to improve the advertising experience on our platform.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Advertising Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, advertising technologies, or legal requirements. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting a notice on our website and updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about our advertising practices.
